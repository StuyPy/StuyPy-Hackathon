What is StuyPy?

StuyPy is a casual, beginner-friendly club aiming to teach people with no prior experience to code in Python, and to create projects while having fun. We additionally want to introduce members to Python, and provide them with a strong foundation in the field of computer science from the ground up.

The StuyPyThon

To better reinforce the concepts we've and allow members to learn even more, We will be hosting StuyPy's first hackathon! Show what you've learned so far, and create a project which you can be proud of! This hackathon is open to all members (anyone can join at the StuyActivities page), where you can work with others or solo if you wish.
